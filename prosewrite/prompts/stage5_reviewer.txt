Review this chapter against all 12 criteria below. For each criterion: assign a score (1–10) and write 1–3 sentences of specific feedback. "Good" is not feedback. Name the specific line, scene, or pattern that earns the score.

1. **POV DISCIPLINE** — Does the narration stay inside the POV character's knowledge and perception? No head-hopping, no omniscient intrusions.

2. **VOICE CONSISTENCY** — Does the prose sound like the same authorial voice throughout? Does each character's dialogue sound distinct from the others?

3. **SCENE MOMENTUM** — Does each scene have a clear power dynamic? Does it move? Is there at least one moment where the situation materially changes?

4. **DIALOGUE QUALITY** — Is every line of dialogue doing at least two things (revealing character AND advancing situation)? Any on-the-nose exposition in dialogue?

5. **SENSORY SPECIFICITY** — Are abstract descriptions replaced by concrete, sensory detail? Flag any paragraph that could have been written without the author having imagined the scene.

6. **PACING** — Does sentence length vary with the emotional register? Is the chapter's internal rhythm appropriate to its genre and content?

7. **CONTINUITY** — Does this chapter contradict any established fact from the story bible, world guide, or character profiles? List any specific contradictions found.

8. **OUTLINE ADHERENCE** — Did the chapter hit the scenes and hook described in the outline? Note any significant deviations.

9. **PROHIBITED PHRASES** — Check for: "suddenly", "little did he/she know", sentences beginning with noticing/realising, adjective stacking, on-the-nose emotional labelling.

10. **WORD COUNT** — Does the draft meet the minimum word count ([[min_words]] words)? If not, where is it thin?

11. **THE HOOK** — Does the final line/scene land? Is it the chapter's most powerful moment? Would a reader stop reading here or turn the page?

12. **ANTI-FLATNESS** — Is the prose personality-rich or generic? Would this chapter be recognisable as this book, or could it have been written by any AI?
