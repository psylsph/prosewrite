## Setting Overview
Where and when does this story take place? Be precise. "Near future" is not precise. "2031, eighteen months after the Shanghai Accord collapsed" is precise.

## Key Locations
For each major location where scenes will take place:
- **Name**: what it is called, any aliases or slang
- **Physical description**: the sensory details that make it feel real
- **Narrative significance**: why this location matters to the story
- **Atmosphere**: what it feels like to be there at different times

## Rules of the World
The constraints that make this world internally consistent:

### Technology
What technology exists that is relevant to the plot? What does NOT exist that might seem plausible? Where are the limits?

### Political & Social Structures
Who holds power? By what mechanism? Who is excluded and why? What are the visible fault lines?

### Military / Institutional (if applicable)
Chain of command, factions, loyalties, protocols that characters operate within or against.

### Economic Realities
Who has money and who doesn't? How does this affect what characters can and cannot do?
