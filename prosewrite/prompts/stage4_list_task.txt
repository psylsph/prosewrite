TASK: CHAPTER HOOKS

⚠ CRITICAL: The SEED DOCUMENT contains specific requirements the author has mandated for this story. Every requirement in the seed — specific scenes, plot events, character moments, world details — must be assigned to a chapter. Do not invent a chapter plan that ignores or sidelines these requirements.

Determine the appropriate number of chapters based on:
- The scope and complexity of the story
- The narrative arc requirements from the story bible
- All the specific scenes/events required by the seed
- Proper pacing and story structure (not too rushed, not too stretched)

Write one hook entry per chapter. Include Chapter 0 (prelude) only if the story requires setup that shouldn't be in Chapter 1.

Format each entry exactly like this:

**Chapter N — Title**
POV: [character name] | Location: [setting]
Purpose: [one line — what changes between the first and last page of this chapter]
100-200 words describing exactly how this chapter ends: the specific situation, image, line of dialogue, or revelation the reader is left with. This must be a hook, reversal, or unresolved question — never a resolution. Name the characters present, the physical setting, and what has just happened or been revealed in the final moments.

---

Write every chapter in order. Do not skip any. After all chapters, write one short paragraph on overall pacing: where is the midpoint, the "all is lost" moment, and when does the climax begin?
